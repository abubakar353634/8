About Me

Self-taught, hands-on, and still climbing.

No agency background, no big team behind me — just a laptop, a lot of late nights, and a genuine love for building stores that work.

How it started

I started learning Shopify development, product research, and competitor analysis in August 2025 — alongside the basics of Facebook Ads and pixel tracking. No courses with shortcuts, mostly trial, error, and a lot of documentation.

The grind before the breakthrough

The first few months were quiet — no orders, no replies, just building skills and refining how I research products and audit competitor stores. That patience paid off when I landed my first real client on Fiverr and delivered ShopSavy, a full smart-home-essentials store with custom conversion features built from scratch.

Where I am now

I'm actively taking on new Shopify projects — store builds, redesigns, and conversion audits — while sharpening a skill that sets me apart: using AI to write and debug custom Liquid, CSS, and HTML so stores can do things a stock theme simply can't.

Where this is going

Short term, I'm targeting underserved niches — including bilingual English/Arabic stores for UAE and Saudi markets. Long term, the goal is a steady stream of international clients and, eventually, a small digital agency built on the same principle I started with: clean, fast, conversion-first stores — no clutter, no shortcuts.

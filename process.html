How I Work

No surprises, no disappearing acts.

Here's exactly what happens after you reach out — step by step.

1. Discovery

We talk through what you're selling, who it's for, and what "done" looks like for you — budget and timeline included.

2. Research & Planning

If needed, I dig into product viability and competitor stores so the build starts with a real strategy, not guesswork.

3. Build

Store structure, theme customization, and any custom Liquid/CSS features come together — with progress updates along the way, not radio silence.

4. Test & Optimize

Every page gets checked on mobile first — speed, layout, checkout flow — before anything goes live.

5. Launch & Handover

You get a walkthrough of the finished store and clear notes on how everything works, so you're never locked out of your own site.

6. After Launch

Quick fixes and small tweaks after delivery are part of the deal — I don't vanish the moment the invoice is paid.
